Some other things

When an element is inside another element one is considered the father and one is considered its child

Being the father the one that encloses the other one, and the child is the one being enclosed

Also, as in real life a father may have more than one child. for example the body of this page has many childs

like the the headers, paragraphs and list shown above. All of them are the body childs and body is his father
